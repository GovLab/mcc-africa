Public institutions need to change how they work, becoming more data-driven, agile, innovative, and capable of tapping expertise from new sources.

The Multi-City Challenge enables committed cities to collectively tackle problems they have been unable to solve on their own.

The Multi-City Challenge helps solving urban problems using the collective wisdom of our communities.

How to Participate?

Multi City Challenge is a process by which cities facing common problems pool expertise from residents to discover and implement innovative solutions.
